This yellow path is the hike from the trailhead to Stop 1.

We will be hiking on the old mine road. When the mine was active in 1907 this wagon road would have connected through to the old Starr's Pass road.

Take the first right you come to. We will join the old mine road and continue going straight. Soon we will see the remains of one of the Old Pueblo Mine tunnels on our left. You can follow along with the video if you get lost.
