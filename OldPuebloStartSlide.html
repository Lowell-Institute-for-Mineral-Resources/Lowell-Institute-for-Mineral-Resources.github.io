Old Pueblo Mine Hike
Welcome to the Old Pueblo Mine hiking tour! This is a guided hiking tour of the ruins of the Old Pueblo Mine located near the Richard E. Genser Starr Pass trailhead in the Tucson Mountains, Tucson, Arizona. You will be guided through a hike along a series of stops that explore the Ruins of the Old Pueblo Mine. This mine was active here from 1907 to 1909. Learners, old and young, can enhance their hike by trying to find all the items on the hike BINGO sheet.
Why is there a mine in the Tucson Mountains?

This guide will offer hints at each point along the way. Each stop will have a short description of what you can see, along with some photos and/or videos for you to watch. By the time you arrive at the Final Stop, you will have a good understanding of why a mine was here.
