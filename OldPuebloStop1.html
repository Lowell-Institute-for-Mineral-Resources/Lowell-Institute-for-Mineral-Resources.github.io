Stop 1
Old Pueblo Mine No. 6 Tunnel Ruins

On your left you can see that someone has dug into the side of the hill here. This is the site of an old mine tunnel, or “drift.” This drift was once over 200ft long. We cannot see the opening today because it was buried on purpose, or “caved.” Because old mines can be dangerous, the entrance to this mine has been caved to prevent people or animals from getting injured. All of the mine entrances we will see today have been caved.

Take a quick look at the rocks here. Are they the same or different from the ones we saw in the parking lot? Why did someone go to all the effort to dig 200 feet into the side of this hill? Think about that as we continue up to stop 2.
